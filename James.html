Illustrations -
James & the Giant Peach

I made a series of handmade illustrations for the short story “James and the Giant Peach” by Roald Dahl.

I experimented with stylized ilustrations, finalizing on a style that I thought complimented the childlike fantasy elements well.

I used watercolour for the illustrations, lined with black liners.

This helped me achiever that classic storybook illustration look.
