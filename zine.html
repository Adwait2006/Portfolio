Zine -
things you DEFINITELY
might be

A hand-illustrated zine, illustrated and written entirely in charcoal.

I tried to use the gritty, dry texture of the medium to achieve the feeling of body hair, and overall physical imperfection.

It is about physical insecurities. It takes a humorous take on some things I feel.

The zine calls the various insecurities insults, and calls them names. Like in the above example, where the illustration of a person with belly rolls is followed by ‘PIG.”.

And in this spread above, where the insult coined is “unibrow monster”.

Neanderthal.
